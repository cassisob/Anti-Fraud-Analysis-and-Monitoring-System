By Cassiano Sobierai

This project is a real-time transaction monitoring system developed for risk analysis and trend tracking. It features interactive charts, transaction status statistics (Approved, Denied by Model, and Denied by Rule), and recent trends, enabling quick identification of potential issues or anomalies. The goal is to provide a clear and dynamic view of transaction flows, facilitating decision-making and risk management.
